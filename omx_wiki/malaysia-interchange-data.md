## Data boundary

data.gov.my Rapid Rail and KTMB files are historical origin-destination ridership data. They are suitable for the station directory and demand analysis only. Do not derive `TransitResult`, scheduled departures, platform timing, or real-time arrival estimates from these files.

## Verified transfer records

The transfer catalog contains these MyRapid-supported interchange records:

- KL Sentral ↔ MRT Muzium Negara, with Kelana Jaya Line and KL Monorail in the KL Sentral complex.
- MRT Pasar Seni ↔ LRT Pasar Seni (Kelana Jaya Line).
- MRT Merdeka ↔ Plaza Rakyat (Ampang / Sri Petaling Lines).
- MRT Maluri ↔ LRT Maluri (Ampang / Sri Petaling Lines).
- MRT Tun Razak Exchange ↔ MRT Kajang / Putrajaya Lines.
- MRT Bukit Bintang ↔ Bukit Bintang Monorail.
